Poems

#1

Drifting through the skies to seek the divine!

I encountered God with eyes so fine!

Not in the heavens, but a gaze so wise!

As He looked back at me with deep ocean eyes!

#2

Take my hand, my love, and come near,

Though your heart may be broken, I'll mend every tear.

With my voice, I'll lift you, no need to stand tall,

follow my call, and we'll rise above all.

Walk with me now, through life’s winding ways,

Beside me forever, through nights and days.

Oh, the light in your eyes, a glimpse of the sky,

A warmth in your touch that brings life alive.

Your heartbeat, my music, the song of my soul,

You’re the piece of my heart that makes me feel whole.

I love you today and I'll love you anew,

Take my hand, my love, let me walk beside you...
